What makes Revelry different

Rules that push characters toward bold, cinematic choices.

Hope and Fear Even rolls fuel player heroics; odd rolls feed the Storyteller's opposition.
Injury-based combat Stress, Serious, Critical, and Lethal injuries make every hit feel dramatic.
Custom spellcraft Keiral magic combines Elements and Components instead of locking casters into lists.
Traits over classes Mix Scholar, Ambassador, Leader, Mercenary, Warrior, and Spellcaster traits freely.
Identity first Goals, fears, bonds, Expertises, and Experiences make the sheet story-ready.
Playtest spirit The game invites tables to forge new options when the story demands it.
